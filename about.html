About Us

Takamul was founded in October 2012 with the vision of enabling organizations of all kinds grasp the tremendous business opportunities presented by the digital age.

The CEO
Basil Al Dossary

Trusted, 12+ Years Experienced & Specialists

Our founder Basil Al Dossary is an active member of the tech community. His experience in business development, product management, and various aspects of digital product development has been recognized in the Kingdom and abroad.

Basil is building a thriving team of developers, agile project managers, data scientists, and support staff that will lead our customers through digitalization programs to greater business success. Although Takamul started from small beginnings in Al-Khobar City in 2012 we have always been driven by the ambition to become one of the top tech firms in the Kingdom.

We began building next-generation mobile and web apps for small and mid-size businesses. Our areas of expertise have expanded over time - and continue to evolve as technology advances. Today, our experienced team has deep knowledge in a wide range of industry 4.0, analytics, and digital solutions which we bring to bear to solve complex business challenges for our customers.
